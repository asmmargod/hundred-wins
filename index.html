Instructions how to play the game

In order to win the game you must reach 100 points. You roll a dice and the score adds up in the current box. If you roll ONE your current score resets, you lose the points you rolled so far during your turn and the other player gets to roll the dice. In order to keep the score you accumulated during your turn you need to press hold, it then adds your current score and then the other player rolls the dice. Reminder, every time one is rolled a player loses their points accumulated during their turn and the other player gets to roll the dice.
